ABOUT US

ACM-W supports, celebrates, and advocates internationally for the full engagement of women in all aspects of computer science. The Manipal chapter of ACM-W wishes to carry forward the vision of ACMW in establishing a society that not only motivates young girls to become zealous programmers but also provides them with adequate resources and the right mentor-ship at every step in their path. We are a research-centric group, striving at inclusion and domain-depth.

Our Mission

Our Mission

Empowerment Through Education

ACM-W Manipal is dedicated to empowering women in computer science and related STEM fields by providing educational resources, mentorship programs, and networking opportunities.

Community Building and Support

ACM-W Manipal fosters a supportive community where women in computing can connect, collaborate, and share experiences, creating a network of support that encourages career growth and personal development.

Promotion of Leadership and Excellence

We strive to recognize and celebrate the achievements of women in computing, promoting leadership roles and excellence in research, innovation, and entrepreneurship.

Our Vision

A Diverse and Inclusive Tech Landscape

ACM-W Manipal envisions a future where women are equally represented and valued in all areas of computing and related STEM fields, contributing to a diverse and inclusive tech landscape that drives innovation and progress.

Inspiring the Next Generation

We envision inspiring and empowering the next generation of women to pursue careers in computing by providing role models, educational resources, and opportunities for hands-on experience, creating a pipeline of talent that reflects the diversity of society.

Continued Growth and Evolution

We envision ACM-W Manipal as a dynamic and evolving organization that adapts to the changing needs of women in computing, continuously expanding its reach, influence, and impact to ensure sustained progress towards gender equality in the field.

Overview of Organization

Overview of Organization

ACM-W Manipal is a student-led organization dedicated to empowering women in technology. Through workshops, networking events, and advocacy initiatives, we strive to create a supportive community where women can develop their skills, build connections, and thrive in the tech industry. Join us in our mission to promote diversity and inclusion in technology!
